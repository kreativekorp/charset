Apple II MouseText to Unicode Mapping

Several Apple II MouseText characters were made available for the first time in Unicode 13.0 with the addition of the Symbols for Legacy Computing block. The only remaining unencoded characters are the two Apple logos, which are trademarked and cannot be encoded. There are several alternate encodings for the two Apple logos.
