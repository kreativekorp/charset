ATASCII to Unicode Mapping

Control characters are shown with a green background with the printable form (made possible by prefixing the character with the escape character) shown underneath. One ATASCII character, the printable form of the "clear screen" control character, was made available for the first time in Unicode 13.0. Some ATASCII characters may be mapped to one of two Unicode characters depending on their use.
