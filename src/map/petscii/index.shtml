PETSCII to Unicode Mapping

Several PETSCII characters were made available for the first time in Unicode 13.0 with the addition of the Symbols for Legacy Computing block. Four PETSCII characters—the horizontal and vertical box drawing lines and the black and white circles—can be mapped to one of two Unicode characters depending on their use.

In the “Unshifted” and “Shifted” columns, the left side shows the glyph that appears on the Commodore PET and the right side shows the glyph that appears on the Commodore 64. In two cases—the backslash or pound sign and the checkerboard fill—these are mapped to different characters.

The PETSCII control characters, $00-$1F and $80-$9F, should be mapped to the Unicode control characters, U+0000—U+001F and U+0080—U+009F.
